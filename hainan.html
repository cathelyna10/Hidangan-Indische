HIDANGAN INDISCHE





Revered as one of Singapore's most iconic dishes, Hainan Chicken Rice is a symphony of simplicity and flavor. The dish consists of succulent poached chicken and fragrant rice, both cooked using the same rich, aromatic chicken broth. The chicken, renowned for its silky-smooth skin and tender meat, is traditionally cooled in an ice bath after poaching, giving it a distinct gelatinous texture. The rice, on the other hand, stands out with its glossy sheen, infused with garlic and ginger, and cooked in the flavorful chicken stock. Complementing the dish is a medley of dipping sauces — often a tangy chili sauce, dark soy, and a fragrant ginger paste. Typically served with slices of cucumber for a refreshing crunch, this dish epitomizes the soul of Singaporean comfort food. Whether savored in hawker centers or upscale restaurants, Hainan Chicken Rice remains a beloved staple, testament to the island nation's rich culinary tapestry.




Singaporean Hainan Chicken



Ingredients:

For the Chicken:

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 lbs)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, smashed
  • 4 cloves garlic, smashed
  • Ice water bath

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 4 cups chicken stock (from boiling the chicken)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

For the Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon chicken stock

Instructions:

  1. Preparing the Chicken:
    1. Rinse the chicken inside and out. Rub with salt to cleanse the skin and rinse thoroughly.
    2. In a large pot, boil water with ginger and garlic. Once boiling, place the chicken in, ensuring it's fully submerged. Boil for around 45 minutes.
    3. Remove chicken and immediately submerge in an ice water bath for 15 minutes to halt the cooking and ensure the skin's gelatinous texture.
    4. Once cooled, rub the chicken with sesame oil for flavor and a glossy finish. Set aside.
  2. Preparing the Rice:
    1. Rinse the rice until water is clear. Drain.
    2. In a pan, heat oil and sauté ginger and garlic until aromatic. Add the drained rice and fry for a couple of minutes.
    3. Transfer the rice mixture to a rice cooker, add chicken stock, and cook.
  3. Preparing the Sauce:
    1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, and chicken stock. Mix well.
  4. Carve the chicken and serve with jasmine rice, sauce, and preferably with some sliced cucumbers on the side.
