Tuscan-style chicken orzo is a mouthwatering Italian-inspired dish that captures the essence of the sun-soaked Tuscan countryside. It begins with tender, juicy chicken breasts, perfectly seared to lock in their natural flavors, and then sliced into generous portions. These succulent chicken pieces are nestled atop a bed of orzo pasta cooked to creamy perfection, infused with a symphony of Mediterranean flavors. A fragrant blend of sun-ripened tomatoes, garlic, basil, and a hint of olive oil creates a luscious sauce that coats every grain of orzo. This harmonious marriage of ingredients results in a comforting and rustic dish that embodies the warmth and simplicity of Tuscan cuisine. With its delightful combination of textures and flavors, Tuscan-style chicken orzo is a culinary journey that transports your taste buds straight to the heart of Italy.




Tuscan Chicken Orzo



Ingredients:

Main Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups orzo pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

    • Calabrian Pepper Topping:

    • 1/4 cup Calabrian chili peppers, finely chopped
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
    • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cooking the Chicken:
    1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and black pepper.
    2. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat.
    3. Add the chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until they are cooked through and no longer pink in the center.
    4. Remove the chicken from the skillet, let it rest for a few minutes, and then slice it into thin strips.
  2. Preparing the Orzo:
    1. Cook the orzo pasta according to the package instructions until it's al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. Making the Tomato Basil Sauce:
    1. In the same skillet used for the chicken, add minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
    2. Add cherry tomatoes and sun-dried tomatoes to the skillet. Cook for 3-4 minutes until the cherry tomatoes soften.
    3. Stir in the cooked orzo and fresh basil leaves. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
    4. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the orzo mixture and stir until it's melted and well incorporated.
  4. Preparing the Calabrian Pepper Topping:
    1. In a small bowl, combine the finely chopped Calabrian chili peppers, olive oil, minced garlic, lemon zest, and a pinch of salt. Mix well.
  5. Serving:
    1. Divide the orzo mixture among serving plates. Top each plate with the sliced chicken.
    2. Drizzle the Calabrian pepper topping over the chicken and orzo.
    3. Garnish with extra fresh basil leaves if desired.
    4. Serve your delicious Tuscan-style chicken orzo with Calabrian pepper topping immediately and enjoy!
