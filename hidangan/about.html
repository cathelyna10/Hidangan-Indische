Saskia takes delight in exploring and cooking a diverse array of world cuisines, infusing each dish with her unique touch. Beyond the kitchen, Saskia's creative flair extends to the digital world. A skilled graphic designer, she's currently furthering her education in graduate school, where she is immersing herself in the intricate world of UX design.
