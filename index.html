Welcome to Hidangan Indische, a culinary endeavor initiated by the passionate and talented Saskia in the vibrant neighborhood of Brooklyn, New York. The soul of our website is the traditional Indonesian cuisine, a cultural treasure that Saskia aims to promote and preserve through her delightful cooking. Hidangan Indische, translating to "Indonesian Dishes," is a canvas where Saskia paints with her exotic ingredients and culinary creativity. Food enthusiasts are welcome to discover a plethora of traditional Indonesian dishes, each telling a story of the archipelago's vibrant history and culture. From the spicy and aromatic rendang to the subtly sweet and savory gado-gado, Saskia ensures that every recipe shared on Hidangan Indische is a symphony of flavors waiting to be explored and enjoyed.
